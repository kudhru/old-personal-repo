I am currently an assistant professor at IIIT Delhi. In the Past, I was a Post-Doc Researcher at Microsoft Research. Prior to Post-doc, I completed my PhD under Prof. Abhishek Chandra in the Computer Science Department at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities. During my PhD, I also collaborated with Prof. Ramesh Sitaraman at University of Massachusetts, Amherst. Broadly, I am interested in Distributed Systems and its application in Data Analytics and Machine Learning.

I graduated in 2014 with a Bachelors in Computer Science from BITS-Pilani, India. At BITS-Pilani, I was a part of Advanced Data Analytics and Parallel Technologies Lab, where I worked on parallel and distributed algorithms for data mining.
